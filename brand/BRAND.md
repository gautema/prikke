# Prikke Brand Guidelines

## Name

**Prikke** - Norwegian for "dot"

From the expression "til punkt og prikke" meaning "to the letter" or "precisely."

## Tagline

> Jobs done, til punkt og prikke

Alternative taglines:
- "Background jobs, done right"
- "Precisely on time"
- "Cron for Europe"

## Logo

The logo consists of:
1. A green dot (â—) - represents precision, the "prikk"
2. The wordmark "prikke" in Inter SemiBold

### Usage
- `logo.svg` - For light backgrounds
- `logo-dark.svg` - For dark backgrounds
- `favicon.svg` - The dot only, for favicons

### Spacing
- Minimum clear space: 1x the height of the dot on all sides
- Dot and text spacing: 0.5em

## Colors

### Primary Palette
| Name | Hex | Usage |
|------|-----|-------|
| Slate 900 | `#0f172a` | Primary text, headings |
| Slate 500 | `#64748b` | Secondary text |
| Emerald 500 | `#10b981` | Accent, the dot, CTAs |

### The Dot
Always use Emerald 500 (`#10b981`) for the dot. This is the brand's signature element.

## Voice & Tone

- **Precise** - Say what you mean, no fluff
- **Friendly** - Approachable, not corporate
- **Confident** - We do one thing well
- **Norwegian** - Subtle nods to origin, not overdone
