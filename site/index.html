What you get

Cron & scheduled jobs

Recurring cron schedules or one-time jobs at a specific time. Run every minute, daily, or just once.

Webhook delivery

How it works

# Recurring job - runs every day at 06:00 UTC curl -X POST https://api.prikke.io/jobs \ -H "Authorization: Bearer pk_live_xxx" \ -d '{ "name": "Daily backup", "url": "https://myapp.com/api/backup", "cron": "0 6 * * *" }' # One-time job - runs once at a specific time curl -X POST https://api.prikke.io/jobs \ -H "Authorization: Bearer pk_live_xxx" \ -d '{ "name": "Send reminder", "url": "https://myapp.com/api/remind", "run_at": "2025-02-01T09:00:00Z" }'
